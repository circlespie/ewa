**Course Description**

This course provides business majors with an introduction to differential and integral calculus. Topics include differentiation, integration, and their applications: derivatives of polynomials, rational, exponential and logarithmic functions; curve sketching; optimization problems; and the definite integral. Applications are drawn from business and economics.
