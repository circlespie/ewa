MATH 1100: Finite Mathematics

Mathematics with applications to probability, statistics, and finance.

Instructor: Erli Wind-Andersen (Instructor)

Term: Fall

Location: Fordham University, FMH 312

Time: TWF, 8:30 AM–9:20 AM

Course Description

This course introduces topics in mathematics with real-world applications: sets and Venn diagrams, counting principles, probability (including conditional probability and Bayes’ theorem), binomial probability, random variables, basic statistics, normal distribution, and an overview of mathematics for finance.
